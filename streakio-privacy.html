Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 20, 2026

StreakIO ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our minimalist habit tracker app collects, uses, and discloses information.

1. Data Collection & Storage

StreakIO is designed with a "Privacy First" approach:

  • Local Storage: All your habits, check-ins, and journal entries are stored 100% locally on your device using an encrypted database.
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not transmit your personal habit data to any external servers or cloud storage.
  • Data Ownership: You fully own your data. You can export it via CSV or delete it entirely within the app settings.

2. App Permissions

To function correctly, StreakIO requires specific permissions:

  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): Used solely to send you habit reminders you have scheduled.
  • Alarms (SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM): Used to ensure your reminders fire at the exact time you requested, even if the device is in doze mode.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not sell your data. However, the app may use standard Android services (like Google Play Services) which may collect anonymous diagnostic data (e.g., crash reports) to help us improve app stability.

4. Your Rights

Since your data is stored locally, you have full control:

  • You can edit or delete any habit or journal entry at any time.
  • You can uninstall the app to permanently remove all data from your device.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
