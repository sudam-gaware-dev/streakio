1. Data Ownership & Storage

StreakIO operates on a "Local-First" architecture. This means:

  • Local Database: All your habits, check-in history, and journal entries are stored exclusively on your device's internal memory (using an encrypted Room Database).
  • No Cloud Sync: I do not maintain a backend server. I do not transmit, store, or view your personal habit data.
  • Data Control: Since the data resides solely on your device, you have complete ownership. If you uninstall the app, your data is permanently deleted from the device.

2. Permissions & Usage

To provide the core functionality of a habit tracker, the app requires specific permissions:

  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): Used solely to deliver the reminders you schedule for your habits.
  • Exact Alarms (SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM): Used to ensure your notifications fire precisely at the set time, regardless of battery optimization.
  • Storage (Read/Write): Used only when you explicitly choose to export your data (PDF/CSV) or import a backup.

3. Third-Party Services

While I do not personally collect your data, the app may use third-party services that may collect information used to identify your device for diagnostic purposes:

  • Google Play Services: Used for app distribution and crash reporting (e.g., if the app crashes, an anonymous log helps me fix the bug).

4. Data Backup & Export

Because I do not store your data on a cloud server, you are responsible for your data backups. StreakIO provides features to Export your data to CSV or PDF. I strongly recommend backing up your data regularly to prevent loss in case of device failure.
